## For Prospective Students

I am always looking for motivated students to join my research group. If you are interested in working with me:

### PhD Applicants
- Please apply through the official university admission process
- Feel free to email me with your CV and research interests
- Include "Prospective PhD Student" in your email subject

### Master's Students
- Contact me after being admitted to discuss potential projects
- Review my research areas and recent publications first

### Undergraduate Researchers
- I welcome undergraduate students interested in research experience
- Send me your CV and describe your interests
